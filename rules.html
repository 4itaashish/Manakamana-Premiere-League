Fixtures Points Table Register Rules

From our group discussion among all the members.
Here is the conclusion and some important rules....

1. Legends and Retired players cannot play
2. All cards can play even they are 110 or 120 rated except point no 1.
3. The tournament will take place in league stage. No groups are divided.
4. One player will play 2 games with each other players.
5. Top 4 teams will advance to knockout.
6. First and second placed team will play each other. whoever wins will go straight to final.
7. Loser from point 6 will play against the winner of 3rd and 4th placed team in the league.
8. loser of 3rd and 4th place team match will be knocked out.
9. Winner of point 7 will play final and ultimate winner will be decided.
10. Prize pool is Rs.600 Bucks for winner and Rs.300 Bucks for 1st runner up.
11. If players are found guilty of using legends and Retired players their league points will be deducted by 3 points.
12. All the games should be finished before 10 pm. If anyone cannot complete the match in particular match day the his opponent will be rewarded a win.
13. All the participants should compulsory pay entry fee before the start of Matchday 1.
14. In the Match between Team A and Team B. If Team A is leading and his internet cuts then the match will be replayed whole no matter the score.
15. In the match between Team A and Team B. If Team A is leading but the Team B internet cuts off then the new game will be played continuing with remaining time and score will continue too.
